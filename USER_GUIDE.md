## ğŸ”§ **Troubleshooting**

### **Common Issues:**

#### **"Can't send messages"**
- **Check internet connection**
- **Refresh the page** (F5)
- **Make sure you're logged in** (check top-right corner)

#### **"Groups not loading"**
- **Wait a few seconds** for data to load
- **Check browser console** (F12) for errors
- **Try refreshing the page**

#### **"Status not uploading"**
- **Check file size** (max 50MB)
- **Supported formats:** JPG, PNG, MP4, MOV
- **Try a smaller file** if upload fails

#### **"User not found"**
- **Double-check email address** spelling
- **User might need to register** first
- **Try adding them again** after they sign up
